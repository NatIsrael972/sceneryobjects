Formats Included:
GIMP (.xcf)
Adobe Photoshop (.psd)

Instructions:

1. Open either in GIMP/Photoshop
2. Paint ONLY above Template
3. Export as .png
5. Replace the existing images in Texture folder of the object.

Tips:

advertpanel.psd -> Advertisement Panel Border and Advertisement
display.psd -> DO NOT EDIT, For text to display on display
displaybg.psd -> Black Display Background
innerwhite.psd -> Inner Panel White Color
outercolor.psd -> Color of the outer Advertisement Panel
